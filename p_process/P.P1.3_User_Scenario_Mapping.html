P1.1: aoikumo User Scenario Mapping

Scenario

Cindy is a college student and wishes to decorate her new backpack for school. She wants cute, quality pins that will make her backpack more personal and unique. Cindy browses the aoikumo website in search of some pins to purchase. She considers the price, product rating, and shipping time while shopping. Cindy chooses the pins that she likes, adds them to her shopping cart and places her order.
